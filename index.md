Hi there! 👋 Nice to meet you.

I am a theoretical high energy physicist turned software developer turned engineering manager — and, as of 2026, a developer again. I've been in the software industry for over 20 years and have had the opportunity to work with technologies across the full stack.

Currently, I work at [Shopify](https://shopify.com) as a Senior Staff Engineer on the Ruby and Rails Infrastructure team, where I focus on hands-on technical work across our Ruby and Rails tooling and infrastructure.

Before that, I was the Engineering Manager of the Ruby Infrastructure team — the team responsible for ensuring that the Ruby language and tooling around it continues to thrive and develop for years to come. Among other things, we worked on building [a high-performance JIT compiler inside CRuby](https://speed.yjit.org), optimizing [the memory layout of CRuby objects for better performance](https://rubykaigi.org/2021-takeout/presentations/peterzhu2118.html), and implementing [object shapes for simpler and faster ivar access](https://rubykaigi.org/2021-takeout/presentations/chrisgseaton.html).

From December 2023 to January 2026, I served on the Board of [Ruby Central](https://rubycentral.org), helping the organization with its OSS and conference efforts.
